Sponsorship Benefits

APNet offers different support package possibilities that can suit organizations of any size. These packages offer an opportunity for the supporting organization, to promote its visibility, services and products to a large community. It is also an opportunity to demonstrate support to research and development activities in the computer networking community.

Support Levels

  • Bronze, USD$5,000
  • Silver, USD$10,000:
  • Gold, USD$20,000
  • Platinum, USD$60,000

Sponsorship Benefits

All sponsors will be listed in the Conference Program, ACM Conference Proceedings and on the Conference Web site, with a link to the sponsor's company Home Page. Depending on the level of sponsorship, a sponsor will receive the following additional benefits

Bronze Sponsor

  • Display space for your company's promotional material at the conference
  • Logo on the conference's web site and online conference platform
  • Logo on official sponsorship signage at the conference
  • Logo on sponsor acknowledgement slide at the conference opening session

Silver Sponsor

  • Display space for your company's promotional material at the conference
  • Logo on the conference's web site and online conference platform
  • A half-page listing on conference’s printed flyer (logo, URL and up to 150-word description) inserted in gift bag to registered attendees
  • Logo on official sponsorship signage at the conference
  • Logo on sponsor acknowledgement slide at the conference opening session
  • One full complimentary conference registration, including all lunches and dinners

Gold Sponsor

  • Display space for your company's promotional material at the conference
  • Logo on the conference's web site and online conference platform
  • One full-page listing on conference’s printed flyer (logo, URL and up to 300-word description) inserted in gift bag to registered attendees
  • Additional (if any) company-provided promotion page or item to be given to all registered attendees
  • Logo on official sponsorship signage at the conference
  • Logo on sponsor acknowledgement slide at the conference opening session
  • Company-provided banner in the conference coffee break area
  • Three full complimentary conference registrations, including all lunches and dinners

Platinum Sponsor

1. Brand Identity Integration

  • Sponsor's corporate logo will be listed on all digital platforms of the conference (official website, registration platform, etc.), with hyperlinks directing to Sponsor's corporate homepage.
  • Sponsor's corporate logo will be displayed on all official sponsor recognition signage within the conference venue.
  • Sponsor's corporate logo will be shown on the sponsor acknowledgment slide during the opening ceremony speech.
  • Single-page promotional materials (content provided by Sponsor) will be included in gift bags distributed to registered attendees, with the option to add additional promotional pages or commemorative items at Sponsor's request.
  • Customized corporate display stands (content provided by Sponsor) will be placed in conference coffee break, lunch, and banquet areas.
  • Sponsor's corporate promotional video (3-4 minutes duration) will be played on loop before the conference and during intermissions.

2. Physical Display Privileges

  • A dedicated exhibition area will be established for Sponsor in the core section of the main conference venue, including a corporate display backdrop, three booths (with location priority for Sponsor), and three LCD screens for displaying promotional materials.
  • A dedicated networking zone will be set up for Sponsor within the main conference venue to facilitate receptions and business meetings.

3. APNet Conference Privileges

  • Sponsor will receive up to six (inclusive) VIP fast-track passes for designated personnel (enterprise leaders above specified levels and experts), enabling priority access to main sessions and banquet events using special credential badges.
  • Sponsor will receive twenty complimentary all-access conference registrations.
  • Sponsor will receive three staff passes for logistical support (e.g., expert receptions), with full access privileges to main sessions and banquet events.
  • Sponsor will receive reserved seating at banquet main and secondary tables (quantity to be mutually agreed), along with speaking opportunities during the conference banquet.
